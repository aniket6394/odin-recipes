chola

This Chole Masala, also known as Chole Recipe, is an authentic North Indian, Punjabi style curry made with white chickpeas, freshly powdered spices, onions, tomatoes and herbs. Naturally vegan and packed with healthy minerals, protein and fiber, this delicious street style Chole Recipe can be ready in just 45 minutes minus the soaking time of chickpeas.

Ingredients are

  • 1 cup driedchickpeas
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • garam masala
  • cardamomm and cloves
  • red chilli powder

Steps are

  1. Prepare Chickpeas: If using dried chickpeas, soak them overnight and cook them in a pressure cooker for 3-4 whistles. If using canned chickpeas, rinse them well.
  2. Cook the Spices: Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and sauté for a minute.
  3. Make the Gravy: Add pureed tomatoes, and cook for 5-7 minutes until the oil separates. Then add turmeric, coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Add Chickpeas: Add the cooked chickpeas to the pan. If needed, add a little water to get a thick gravy. Cook for 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  5. Finishing Touches: Add chole masala and garam masala, and cook for another 5 minutes. Garnish with fresh cilantro.
